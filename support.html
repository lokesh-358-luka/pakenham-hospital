How do I book an appointment? ▼

You can book an appointment through several methods:

Online: Use our patient portal or website booking system available 24/7

Phone: Call our appointment line at (03) 9594-5000 during business hours

Mobile App: Download our mobile app for convenient booking

In Person: Visit our reception desk during hospital hours

What insurance plans do you accept? ▼

We accept most major insurance plans including:

• Medicare and Medicaid

• Private health insurance (Bupa, Medibank, HCF, etc.)

• Workers' compensation

• Motor vehicle accident claims

Please contact our billing department at (03) 9594-5000 to verify your specific coverage.

When should I go to the emergency room? ▼

Visit the emergency room immediately for:

• Chest pain or difficulty breathing

• Severe injuries or trauma

• Signs of stroke or heart attack

• Severe allergic reactions

• Loss of consciousness

• Severe bleeding

For life-threatening emergencies, call 000 immediately.

What are your visiting hours? ▼

General Visiting Hours:

• Monday to Sunday: 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM

ICU Visiting Hours:

• Daily: 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM and 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM

Maternity Ward:

• Partners: 24/7 access

• Other visitors: 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Please note that visiting hours may be restricted during certain circumstances.

How can I access my medical records? ▼

You can access your medical records through:

Patient Portal: Login to your online account to view test results, medications, and visit summaries

Mobile App: Download our app for convenient access to your health information

Request Copies: Contact Medical Records at (03) 9594-5000 to request printed copies

You'll need to provide valid identification and may need to complete a release form.

How do I cancel or reschedule my appointment? ▼

To cancel or reschedule your appointment:

Online: Use your patient portal or our website

Phone: Call (03) 9594-5000 at least 24 hours in advance

Mobile App: Use our app's appointment management feature

Please provide at least 24 hours notice to avoid cancellation fees and to allow other patients to use the appointment slot.

How can I pay my medical bills? ▼

We offer several convenient payment options:

Online: Pay through our secure patient portal

Phone: Call our billing department at (03) 9594-5000

Mail: Send check or money order to our billing address

In Person: Pay at our reception desk

We accept cash, checks, credit cards, and offer payment plans for larger amounts.
